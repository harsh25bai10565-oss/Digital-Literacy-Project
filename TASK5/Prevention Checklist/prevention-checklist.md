Prevention Checklist

UPI Rule #1: Remember that a UPI PIN is only needed to send money, never to receive it.

Verify QR Codes: Never scan a QR code sent by a stranger on WhatsApp or OLX.

Enable App Lock: Always use a separate PIN or biometric lock for your payment apps (Paytm, GPay, etc.).

Check URLs: Do not click on suspicious links in SMS or emails that claim you have won a lottery or reward.

Use Official Apps: Download apps only from the official Play Store or App Store.

Public Wi-Fi Warning: Avoid conducting bank transactions while connected to free public Wi-Fi.

Limit Information: Don't share your ATM PIN, OTP, or CVV with anyone, even if they claim to be a bank official.

Report Immediately: If defrauded, report the incident on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Helpline 1930.
