# Social Media Checklist - Harsh Gabrani

## Do's:
* Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
* Double-check facts before sharing any news or links.
* Keep your LinkedIn profile professional and updated.
* Network with alumni and industry professionals politely.
* Think for 30 seconds before hitting the 'Post' button.

## Don'ts:
* Don't post sensitive personal info like your home address.
* Don't engage in online arguments or "cyber-flaming."
* Don't use offensive language or unprofessional photos.
* Don't post complaints about your professors or college.
* Don't tag people in photos without their permission.
